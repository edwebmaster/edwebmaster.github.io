Design

Esposito Design is a residential design and consulting service. We work with individuals and homebuilders to craft thoughtful, beautiful home designs.

Form, Meet Function

Our designs are grounded in essential principles of design. Massing and proportion are brought together with common-sense space arrangements to create home designs that just work.

 

​View Our Portfolio

Design in Three Dimensions

Sometimes what looks great in 2D or on paper doesn't translate into the real world very well. That's why every one of our designs are drawn in Autodesk Revit®—an industry leading design documentation software that enables our clients to explore design options in 3D space prior to building. Our process helps you to visualize your home and communicate design intent to builders.
